# Code of Conduct

Qadamha exists to honor people's effort. The same spirit governs how we treat
each other here.

## Our standards

- **Respect the people in the stories.** Founders and teams trusted this
  archive with painful chapters of their lives. No mockery, no blame, no
  "they failed because they were bad at X" commentary.
- **Respect each other.** Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and
  hate speech are not tolerated — in issues, pull requests, or story content.
- **All three languages are equal.** Never dismiss a contribution because of
  the language it's written in.
- **Assume good faith.** Many contributors are writing in their second or
  third language, or using GitHub for the first time. Help them.
- **Stay honest.** Deliberately false stories or defamation will be removed.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content and comments that violate these
standards, and block repeat offenders. To report a problem privately, email
the maintainers at qadamha@lajward.dev.
